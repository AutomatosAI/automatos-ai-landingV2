Conditions we treat

Cannabis is a tool, not a panacea. These are the indications where evidence and our clinical experience align.

When other analgesics have run their course

For neuropathic pain, fibromyalgia, and treatment-resistant chronic pain. Our prescribers favour balanced THC:CBD formulations and titrate slowly. Most patients report meaningful improvement within four to six weeks; some sooner. We track outcomes at every follow-up.

Chronic insomnia and sleep architecture

Indica-leaning flower and oils, dosed in the evening, can restore sleep onset and continuity for patients where standard hypnotics have failed or caused dependency. Sleep diaries are part of our protocol.

When SSRIs are not the answer

CBD-dominant formulations, sometimes with low-dose THC, can support generalised anxiety and PTSD-related symptoms in patients who have not tolerated or responded to first-line treatments. Always alongside, never instead of, talking therapy.

Refractory epilepsy and MS spasticity

Where the evidence is strongest — pharmaceutical CBD for treatment-resistant childhood epilepsies, and THC:CBD oromucosal sprays for multiple-sclerosis spasticity. Always co-managed with the patient's neurologist.

The practice

A clinic, not a dispensary.

Apothicare was founded in Lisbon in 2022 by Dr Elena Marques, a consultant in pain medicine, after a decade of patients asking the same question: where do I get this safely, in Portugal, from a doctor? We answered with a practice — proper consultations, INFARMED-authorised imports, pharmaceutical-grade product, and follow-up care. We are not a delivery app. We are a small clinical team that happens to dispense cannabis when it is the right tool for the patient in front of us.

Questions

Questions we answer most often

If something here is unclear, write to us. A real clinician will reply, usually within a working day.

Is medical cannabis legal in Portugal? +
Yes — INFARMED, the Portuguese medicines authority, has authorised medical cannabis prescribing since 2019. Apothicare operates under a full INFARMED licence for import, dispensing, and patient care. Recreational cannabis remains illegal.
Do I need a referral from my doctor? +
No referral is required. We do, however, ask you to bring relevant medical records to your consultation — diagnoses, medication lists, previous treatments tried. Where appropriate, we will write to your existing physician with your consent.
What does treatment cost? +
The eligibility check is free. The initial consultation is €185 and includes 45 minutes with a prescribing physician. Quarterly follow-ups (€95) are included in your annual care plan. Medication is priced separately and varies by formulation; expect €120–€280 per month at typical prescribed doses.
Will it make me high? +
Some formulations contain THC and may produce psychoactive effects, particularly during titration. Many patients are prescribed CBD-dominant or balanced products specifically to minimise this. Your prescriber will discuss what to expect at the formulation chosen for you, and titrate from a low dose.
Can I drive while on treatment? +
Driving under the influence of THC is illegal in Portugal regardless of prescription status. Patients on THC-containing medication should not drive within four hours of a dose, and should discuss long-term driving implications with their prescriber. CBD-only formulations do not affect driving ability.
Is my information confidential? +
Yes. We are bound by the same medical confidentiality and GDPR obligations as any Portuguese clinic. Records are held on encrypted EU-resident systems. We never share patient data with employers, insurers, or third parties without your explicit written consent.
