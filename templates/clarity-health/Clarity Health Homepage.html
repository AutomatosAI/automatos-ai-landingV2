FAQ

Honest answers to the questions we hear most

Can't see yours? Message a clinician — we reply within one working day.

Is medical cannabis legal in the UK?
Yes. Cannabis-based medicinal products have been legal to prescribe in the UK since November 2018, when prescribed by a specialist consultant on the GMC Specialist Register.
Will I get high?
No, not when used correctly. Dosing is titrated slowly with your clinician, and many patients are prescribed CBD-dominant or balanced products that are not intoxicating at therapeutic doses.
How much does treatment cost?
Initial consultation £150; follow-ups £75. Medication is dispensed by pharmacy at cost — typically £150–£300 per month depending on prescription.
Can I drive?
A statutory medical defence applies if you are following your prescription correctly. Your clinician will discuss this and provide written guidance.
Will it affect my employer or insurance?
Treatment is private and confidential. Records are held securely and never shared without your consent.
