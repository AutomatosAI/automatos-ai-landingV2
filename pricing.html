Plate / FAQ Honest answers
The questions that actually come up before sign-up.
↗  Email hello@automatos.ai for the rest

Is the open-source really the same as the hosted?

Yes. The repo at github.com/AutomatosAI/automatos-ai is the production codebase. The hosted plans add managed infrastructure, support, and compliance — not feature gates.

Do I pay for model usage?

No — you bring your own model keys. Automatos is a workforce platform, not a model proxy. The router will pick the cheapest competent model for each task; the bill goes to you, from the provider.

What happens at the agent / mission limit?

Agents over the limit are paused — they keep their config and history. Missions over the limit are queued for the next billing cycle. We never silently overcharge.

Can I move from hosted to self-host?

Either direction. Export your agents, knowledge bases, and routing rules as a single bundle; import on the other side. Same schemas, same code.

What's the EU residency story?

Pro and Enterprise can pin their workspace to our Frankfurt cluster. All data — postgres, vectors, logs — stays in-region. EU AI Act risk-tier audit available on request.

Is there a non-profit / academic plan?

Yes — Pro is free for registered non-profits and accredited research groups. Drop us a line with your domain and we'll switch you over.
